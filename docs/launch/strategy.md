# Cloudrix Launch Strategy — 30-Day Distribution Plan

**Constraint honored:** solo founder with a full-time job. Every day is 30–60 min. Nothing here requires ad spend or a team.

**Positioning (use everywhere):** Solo senior engineer in Tilburg, NL. EU AI Act *implementation engineering* — not legal advice, not a law firm memo. Free scanner as the wedge. Honest about being new — that IS the angle ("I build the compliance tooling myself, you talk to the engineer, not an account manager").

## Regulatory facts — the ONLY version to use

- Digital Omnibus on AI: proposed 19 Nov 2025, provisional agreement 7 May 2026, Parliament endorsed 16 Jun 2026, Council final approval 29 Jun 2026.
- **High-risk (Annex III, stand-alone) obligations: delayed from 2 Aug 2026 → 2 Dec 2027.**
- **High-risk embedded in regulated products (Annex I): → 2 Aug 2028.**
- Art. 50(2) watermarking/marking for providers: postponed to 2 Dec 2026.
- Everything else stays: prohibitions + AI literacy (live since Feb 2025), GPAI rules (Aug 2025), most transparency obligations from 2 Aug 2026.
- Fines unchanged: up to €35M / 7% global turnover for prohibited practices.
- **Never** use "5 weeks away" / "August 2026 deadline" urgency framing (the old `social-media-launch-package.md` at repo root is full of it — do not reuse that file's claims; it also claims "2 years, 50+ countries, 24 tools" — none of that matches reality and it will destroy trust on HN/Reddit).

## Week 0 priorities (Days 1–3): claim the name, fix the foundation

There are **6+ other "Cloudrix" entities** (hosting companies, an IT firm, etc.). Every day unclaimed is a day someone else owns your search results.

- **Day 1 (60 min):** Claim handles: LinkedIn Company Page "Cloudrix" (or "Cloudrix.io" if taken), X @cloudrix / @cloudrixio, GitHub org `cloudrix` / `cloudrix-io`, plus Bluesky and YouTube handles (5 min each, do it once). Set logo + one-line description on all. Google "cloudrix" and note which collisions rank — your differentiator string is "Cloudrix — EU AI Act engineering, Netherlands".
- **Day 2 (45 min):** Personal LinkedIn profile update: headline = "EU AI Act implementation engineer | Founder, Cloudrix (cloudrix.io)". Founder profile is the channel — company pages get ~10% of the reach of personal posts.
- **Day 3 (45 min):** HN account prep. If your account has near-zero karma, a Show HN can be auto-flagged (documented cases in 2026 of zero-karma Show HN removals). Start commenting genuinely on AI/EU-regulation threads now — you need ~2 weeks of light activity before launch.

## Week 1 (Days 4–10): directories + newsjack window

The Omnibus was finalized 29 June 2026 — the newsjack window is still open but closing. All current coverage is law-firm client alerts (DLA Piper, Gibson Dunn, Covington). **The content gap: nobody is writing the engineer's version — "what do I actually reschedule in my roadmap."** That's yours.

- **Day 4 (60 min):** Publish site blog post: "The EU AI Act deadline moved to Dec 2027. Here's what that actually changes in your engineering roadmap." (Reuse for LinkedIn post #1 in `linkedin-posts.md`.)
- **Day 5 (30 min):** Post LinkedIn newsjack post #1. Then 10 min commenting on other people's Omnibus posts (lawyers post these constantly — add the engineering angle in comments; this is where compliance buyers lurk).
- **Day 6 (45 min):** Directory submissions, batch 1: **Clutch.co** profile (free tier; category: AI consulting, Netherlands), **GoodFirms** (free listing). Be accurate: solo, founded 2026. Clutch reviews come later from first clients.
- **Day 7 (30 min):** Directory batch 2: **G2** (list the free scanner as a product under "AI Governance" category — G2 is product-oriented, the scanner qualifies; the consultancy doesn't), Product Hunt upcoming page ("Coming soon" teaser), plus free NL-specific: Techleap ecosystem listing, KvK profile completeness, Google Business Profile (Tilburg).
- **Days 8–10 (30–45 min/day):** LinkedIn cadence begins: post 3×/week (Tue/Wed/Thu mornings CET), comment 10–15×/day (5–10 min, do it on the train/lunch). Commenting on lawyers' and AI-governance posts is the highest-ROI activity for a zero-audience account. Post LinkedIn posts #2–3.

## Week 2 (Days 11–17): Reddit groundwork + PH prep

- **Days 11–13 (30 min/day):** Reddit karma-building — comment helpfully in target subs (see `reddit-plan.md`), zero links. LinkedIn posts #4–5.
- **Day 14 (60 min):** Product Hunt prep: gallery images (4–6 screenshots of the scanner + one results-report shot), finalize copy from `product-hunt.md`, line up 10–15 real acquaintances (ex-colleagues, NL dev friends) who'll genuinely try the tool on launch morning — spread across the day, not midnight-dumped.
- **Days 15–17:** LinkedIn posts #6–7. First Reddit value post (no product link) per `reddit-plan.md`. Continue HN commenting.

## Week 3 (Days 18–24): launches

- **Day 18 (Tuesday): Product Hunt launch.** Take a day off or a WFH day if you can — you need to answer every comment within the first hours. Launch 12:01 AM PT = 9:01 AM CET, which actually suits a NL founder: your morning is PH's critical early window. Checklist in `product-hunt.md`.
- **Day 19:** PH follow-ups: thank commenters, LinkedIn post #8 ("what launching on PH taught me"), reply to stragglers.
- **Day 20–21 (Tue–Thu, 15:00–18:00 CET = 9–12 ET): Show HN.** Post from personal account per `show-hn.md`. Stay near keyboard for 3 hours to answer comments. Do NOT post PH and HN the same day.
- **Days 22–24:** Reddit post #2 (tool feedback post in an appropriate sub, per rules). LinkedIn post #9.

## Week 4 (Days 25–30): outreach engine

- **Days 25–27 (45 min/day):** Cold outreach begins: 5 personalized emails/DMs per day to NL/EU AI SMBs using `outreach.md`. Personalization takes ~7 min each — that's the point; 5 good ones beat 50 blasts.
- **Day 28:** LinkedIn post #10 (solo-founder honest post — save it for when you have small proof points from the launches: "X people ran the scanner").
- **Day 29 (30 min):** Secondary directories: There's An AI For That, Futurepedia, AlternativeTo (list the free scanner), Uneed/Peerlist launch (low effort, backlinks).
- **Day 30 (45 min):** Review: scanner runs, email signups, calls booked. Double down on the one channel that produced conversations. Set month-2 cadence: LinkedIn 3×/week + 5 outreach/day is sustainable indefinitely at ~40 min/day.

## Success metrics (realistic for month 1, zero audience)

- 300–1,000 scanner runs (mostly from HN/PH spikes)
- 5–10 inbound conversations, 1–2 Quick Scan (€2,500) proposals sent
- LinkedIn: 200–500 new relevant followers
- Brand handles secured everywhere (binary: done/not done)
- Do NOT expect: PH top-5, HN front page, or closed implementation deals in month 1. One €2,500 Quick Scan sold = the funnel works.
