## Pricing Strategy

### Model
- **Hybrid pricing** (base subscription + usage/credits) for AI products
- **Per-seat** for collaboration tools (CRM, project management)
- **Flat fee** for engineering tools
- **Commission** for marketplace products

### Billing
- Monthly and annual (20% discount for annual)
- USD and EUR currencies
- 14-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans
- Free tier or 14-day trial on every product

### Regional Pricing
| Tier | Regions | Discount |
|------|---------|----------|
| Tier 1 (Full) | US, UK, Germany, France, Nordics, Australia, Japan | 0% |
| Tier 2 (Moderate) | Southern EU, Israel, UAE, Saudi, South Korea, Singapore | 10-20% |
| Tier 3 (Significant) | Eastern EU, Turkey, Brazil, South Africa, Malaysia | 30-55% |
| Tier 4 (Deep) | India, Indonesia, Nigeria, Kenya, Egypt, Pakistan | 50-70% |
